Welcome to yvm, the questionable JVM.

Yvm executes (some) JVM bytecode.

Yvm Features:

  • Supports Integers. Who needs more than bits anyway?.
  • Supports code in static block and main. Who needs calling methods anyway?
  • Supports 12 bytecode instructions. Who needs dup2_x2 anyway?
  • Supports dumping the interpreter state at end of execution. Who needs more I/O anyway?
